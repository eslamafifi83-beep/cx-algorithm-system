THE CX ALGORITHM ADMIN PANEL

Hero

The first thing visitors see: the big headline, tagline, and intro paragraph.

Wrap a word in *asterisks* to color it gold, e.g. Where *Creativity*

Guest Companies

The scrolling banner under the hero. Each company shows its logo (recolored white automatically); without a logo the name appears as outlined text.

Episodes

Click an episode to expand and edit it. Each episode gets its own page with a Video/Audio player: paste a Spotify link or upload the media file directly. The listing page and homepage cards update automatically.

Blog

A curated index of your LinkedIn articles. Every card opens the article on LinkedIn; a video thumbnail plays silently as a preview only. One article can be flagged as featured.

Messages

Submissions from the contact page: speaking enquiries, guest pitches and general messages. These live on the server only — visitors can't see them.

Host

Your name, bio, and photo, shown in the hero and the "Meet your host" band.

About page

The stats strip, the "On stage" conference photo gallery, and the "As seen at" venue names on the About page. Visitors only ever see the photos you upload here.

Links

Podcast platforms and social profiles, used across the footer.
